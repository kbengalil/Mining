# Rick Rule — Top 10 Investor Questions for a Mining Company

1. What keeps management up at night — and what are they doing about it?
2. What important question haven't I asked yet?
3. Does management have prior success in this specific commodity, geology, and jurisdiction?
4. Why was each director chosen — what unique skill do they bring?
5. Does the whole organization have serial success, not just one person?
6. Is there a compound operator (understands both geology and capital markets)?
7. Does the deposit style match known producing mines?
8. Is the resource open at depth or along strike?
9. Can management defend the project through a 50% share price decline?
10. Can I independently verify enough data to form my own opinion of value?
